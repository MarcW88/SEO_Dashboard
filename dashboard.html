Methodology & Scope

■ Coverage metrics (count-based, from rankings)
Ranked Coverage = keywords with any position / total keywords in panel.
Top 10 Coverage = keywords in position 1–10 / total keywords in panel.
Top 10 Share vs Competitors = domain's Top 10 count / sum of all domains' Top 10 counts.
Source: positions retrieved weekly via SERP API on the 320 tracked keywords.

■ Share of Voice (traffic-based, from panel)
SEO SoV = ∑ est. clicks(domain, keyword) / ∑ est. clicks(all domains, all keywords).
Per keyword: est. clicks = search volume × CTR(position).
This is calculated on the tracked panel only (320 keywords), not on global domain traffic.
Source: volumes via DataForSEO keyword data; positions via SERP API; CTR curve applied per position.

Panel: 320 keywords · Market: Belgium NL · Competitors: spie.be, honeywell.com, vinci-energies.be, siemens.be, se.com
Frequency: Weekly · CTR model: Pos 1 ≈ 30%, Pos 2 ≈ 15%, Pos 3 ≈ 10%, Pos 4–10 declining, Pos 11+ ≈ 0%

Note: Equans currently ranks on only 30 of 320 keywords. SoV will increase as content strategy matures.
